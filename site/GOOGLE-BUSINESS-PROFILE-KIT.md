# Google Business Profile Kit

Use this only if the business is eligible.

Important:

- Google says a Business Profile requires in-person contact with customers during stated hours.
- Google says lead generation agents/companies and online-only businesses are not eligible.
- Service-area businesses can define service areas and hide the address if they serve customers at customer locations.

If your operating model does not qualify, do not force it.

## Category Strategy

Choose the most accurate primary category available at setup.

Start with one primary category only.

Candidate categories to evaluate in Google’s category selector:

- Marketing consultant
- Internet marketing service
- Advertising agency
- Website designer
- Software company

Choose the one that best matches the real business model, not the most aggressive keyword.

If Google allows secondary categories and they are accurate, consider:

- Internet marketing service
- Website designer
- Marketing consultant

Do not add irrelevant categories.

## Review Request Flow

Use only with real clients.

1. deliver or confirm outcome
2. send WhatsApp or email request
3. send direct review link
4. do not script the review
5. reply to every real review

Use `REVIEW-REQUEST-TEMPLATES.md`.
