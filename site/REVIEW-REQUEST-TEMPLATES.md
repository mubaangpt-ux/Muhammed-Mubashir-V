# Review Request Templates

Use these only with real clients, collaborators, or partners.

## WhatsApp - short

Hi [Name], thanks again for working with me on [project]. If the work was useful, would you mind leaving a short review about the outcome and your experience? It helps a lot with trust and future clients. I can send the review link here.

## WhatsApp - after a result

Hi [Name], now that [result/outcome] is live, could you leave a quick review mentioning the project type and how the process felt from your side? A short honest review is enough. I can send the direct link.

## Email - simple

Subject: Quick review request

Hi [Name],

Thank you again for trusting me with [project].

If you are happy with the work, I would appreciate a short honest review. It helps future clients understand the kind of projects I do and how I work.

If helpful, you can mention:

- the kind of problem you needed solved
- what changed after the work
- how the process and communication felt

I can send the direct review link if needed.

Best,
Muhammed Mubashir V

## Email - stronger prompt

Subject: Small favour after the project

Hi [Name],

I am building the public proof around my work more carefully this year.

If the project delivered value, would you be open to leaving a short review? A few lines about the work, the process, and the result is enough.

Helpful themes:

- digital marketing / SEO / web development / automation
- speed and clarity of communication
- measurable improvement or operational usefulness

Thanks,
Muhammed Mubashir V

## Review prompt points

Do not script the review for the client. If they ask what to include, suggest these points:

- what the business needed
- what was built or improved
- what changed afterward
- whether communication and delivery were clear

## Rules

- do not incentivize reviews
- do not ask non-clients for reviews
- do not gate unhappy clients
- do not post fake reviews
