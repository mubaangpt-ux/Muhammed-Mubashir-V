# Backlink Targets

This file is the practical backlink and mention target list for `mubaan.online`.

The goal is not raw count.

The goal is:

- relevant referring domains
- Dubai / UAE relevance
- service-buyer relevance
- profiles and mentions that AI assistants can discover and verify

## Rules

- No paid link schemes.
- No PBNs.
- No comment spam.
- No fake guest posts on low-quality domains.
- No directory spam.

## Tier 1: Foundational Public Profiles

These are not editorial backlinks, but they are high-value discovery and corroboration signals.

1. LinkedIn public profile
2. Bing Places / Bing for Business
3. Google Business Profile if eligible
4. GoodFirms
5. Clutch
6. Upwork

Primary destination URL:

- `/freelance-digital-marketer-dubai`

Secondary URLs:

- `/press-kit`
- `/work`

## Tier 2: Relationship-Based Links

These usually convert better than cold editorial outreach.

Targets:

- past clients
- current clients
- agency partners
- production collaborators
- developers or designers you worked with
- vendors and suppliers

Ask for:

- a partner page mention
- a “worked with” or “collaborators” mention
- a credited case-study link
- an implementation credit on a project page

Best destination URLs:

- `/work`
- specific case study URLs
- `/press-kit`

- alumni pages

- coworking, accelerator, or incubator directories
- Dubai-focused freelancer or media communities
