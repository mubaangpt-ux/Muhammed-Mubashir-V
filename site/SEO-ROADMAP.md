# SEO and GEO Roadmap

## What is already implemented in code

- Canonical URLs, robots directives, Open Graph, Twitter cards, and manifest.
- Structured data for `WebSite`, `Person`, `ProfessionalService`, `Service`, `FAQPage`, `CollectionPage`, and `BreadcrumbList`.
- Generated `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`.
- Dubai-focused service hub and 3 dedicated service landing pages.
- Added internal links from home, work, footer, resume, about, and case studies.
- Added `llms.txt` for AI-readable site discovery.
- Added env hooks for Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster verification.

## External tools to connect next

- Google Search Console
- Bing Webmaster Tools
- Google Analytics 4
- Google Business Profile
- Rich Results Test
- PageSpeed Insights
- Ahrefs or Semrush
- IndexNow

## Phase 1: Verification and indexing

- Set `PUBLIC_SITE_URL` to the exact production domain.
- Add `PUBLIC_GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION`.
- Add `PUBLIC_BING_SITE_VERIFICATION`.
- Submit `/sitemap.xml` in Google Search Console.
- Submit `/sitemap.xml` in Bing Webmaster Tools.
- Request indexing for `/`, `/services`, each service page, `/work`, and featured case studies.

## Phase 2: Local SEO for Dubai

- Finalize business name, phone, email, and Dubai service-area wording consistently across the site.
- Create or optimize Google Business Profile.
- Add Dubai-specific images, service descriptions, and categories in GBP.
- Build citations on high-quality UAE and Dubai directories only.
- Collect legitimate reviews tied to actual service delivery.

## Phase 3: Authority and link acquisition

- Publish more case studies with measurable outcomes.
- Add one new local insight or guide per month around Dubai marketing, SEO, or lead generation.
- Earn links from partners, suppliers, industry associations, UAE directories, and client mentions.
- Link from owned profiles where relevant: Instagram, LinkedIn, business listings, portfolio profiles.

## Phase 4: Content expansion

- Add city-intent supporting pages only where there is real service relevance.
- Expand each service page with fresh proof, screenshots, FAQs, and project references.
- Build topic clusters around digital marketing Dubai, technical SEO Dubai, and lead generation Dubai.

## Phase 5: Measurement

- Track clicks, impressions, coverage, and queries in Search Console.
- Track conversions by landing page, source, and service page in GA4.
- Review internal-link coverage monthly.
- Refresh underperforming pages every 30 to 45 days.

## Notes

- First-page rankings cannot be guaranteed.
- Technical SEO creates eligibility and clarity. Rankings still depend on competition, domain trust, backlinks, and content depth.
- Do not add spammy outbound links or low-quality directory links just to increase link count.
