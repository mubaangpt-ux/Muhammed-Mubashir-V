# AI Shortlist Playbook

This file is specifically for queries like:

- best digital marketer in Dubai
- top digital marketers in Dubai
- digital marketing freelancer in Dubai
- available digital marketer in Dubai

## What the user-provided ChatGPT output shows

The shortlist logic is not based on one website alone.

The model appears to do two separate jobs:

1. discover candidate names from strong public sources
2. verify whether those names look real, relevant, and possibly hireable

In the sample output, the names came from sources such as:

- editorial or award coverage
- public profile pages
- review platforms
- LinkedIn
- freelancer or consultant websites

## What this means for Mubaan

Your website helps with verification.

Third-party profiles and mentions help with discovery.

You need both.

## Priority Actions

### 1. Make availability explicit

Done in this repo:

- `/freelance-digital-marketer-dubai`
- `/press-kit`

These pages help AI systems verify that you are an individual, independent, available option in Dubai.

### 2. Build the public profile stack

Create and fully complete:

- LinkedIn profile
- Google Business Profile, if eligible
- Bing Places / Bing Webmaster
- Clutch
- GoodFirms
- Upwork or another credible marketplace if that fits your business model

All profiles should point to the same website, same name, same phone, same email, and same Dubai location.

### 3. Earn third-party mentions

Aim for:

- interviews
- podcast guest pages
- partner pages
- supplier / collaborator pages
- local business communities
- event pages
- editorial contributions

The model is more likely to shortlist names that appear on citable third-party pages.

### 4. Keep the first-party site stronger than generic freelancer sites

The site now has:

- role pages
- service pages
- proof pages
- guides
- press kit
- official identity links

Keep adding:

- real case studies
- result-specific proof
- public project summaries
- clear availability signals

### 5. Collect real reviews

Reviews will not force ChatGPT inclusion by themselves, but they help trust, local prominence, and profile credibility.

Use `REVIEW-REQUEST-TEMPLATES.md`.

## What not to do

- do not create fake awards
- do not post fake reviews
- do not publish self-made top 10 lists
- do not spam low-quality directory profiles
- do not buy backlinks

## Outcome Goal

The goal is not to trick AI into saying number 1.

The goal is to make the model see:

- a real named individual
- based in Dubai
- with an accessible website
- clearly available for work
- with service depth
- with proof
- with third-party corroboration
